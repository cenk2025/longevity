Cookie Policy

Last Updated: December 19, 2025

Your Privacy Matters: We use cookies to improve your experience on Longevity IQ. This policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can control them in accordance with Finnish and EU law.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience.

We use cookies and similar technologies (such as local storage, session storage, and pixels) in compliance with:

  • EU ePrivacy Directive (2002/58/EC)
  • Finnish Information Society Code (917/2014)
  • EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, authentication, and session management.

Legal basis: Legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR) - these cookies are necessary for the service you requested.

No consent required under Finnish law as they are strictly necessary.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
sb-access-token Authentication and session management Session
sb-refresh-token Maintain user login state 30 days
cookie-consent Remember your cookie preferences 1 year

2.2 Functional Cookies

These cookies allow us to remember your preferences and provide enhanced features.

Legal basis: Consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR)

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
user-preferences Store your display and language preferences 1 year
theme-mode Remember your light/dark mode preference 1 year

2.3 Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.

Legal basis: Consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR)

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
_analytics_session Track user sessions and page views 30 minutes
_analytics_user Distinguish unique users (anonymized) 2 years

2.4 Performance Cookies

These cookies collect information about how you use our website to help us improve performance.

Legal basis: Consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR)

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
performance-metrics Monitor page load times and errors Session

5. Your Cookie Choices

5.1 Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, we will ask for your consent to use non-essential cookies. You can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Customize your cookie preferences

5.2 Managing Cookies

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time by:

  • Clicking the button below to open cookie settings
  • Using your browser settings to block or delete cookies
  • Visiting our Privacy Settings page

5.3 Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. Here's how:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

Note: Blocking all cookies may affect website functionality.

6. Cookie Retention

Different cookies have different retention periods:

  • Session cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies: Remain for a set period (see tables above)
  • Analytics data: Anonymized after 26 months (GDPR requirement)

7. Local Storage and Similar Technologies

In addition to cookies, we use:

7.1 Local Storage

HTML5 local storage to save:

  • User preferences (theme, language)
  • Cached data for offline functionality
  • Test progress and results

7.2 Session Storage

Temporary storage for:

  • Form data during active sessions
  • Navigation state

7.3 IndexedDB

For storing larger amounts of structured data locally, such as:

  • Offline test data
  • Cached API responses

8. Do Not Track (DNT)

We respect Do Not Track (DNT) browser settings. If you enable DNT:

  • We will not set analytics or performance cookies
  • We will only use strictly necessary cookies
  • Third-party analytics will be disabled
